Volunteer Experience

I'm Vincentian, a volunteer member of Society of Saint Vincent de Paul (SSVP). SSVP is a non-governmental organization under French law (non-profit organization under the law of 1901). Its head office is located in Paris, France.

The SSPV is present on all 5 continents, in 153 locations (each with its own Council). It has 800,000 active members and over one million volunteers in local teams called “Conferences”, forming a vast network of Charity around the world. The Conferences are united by a fraternal bond, offering mutual support to each other through prayer, twinnage relationships or material assistance. Councils are the authorities responsible for maintaining unity with Paris, the headquarters of the International Council General of the Society of Saint Vincent de Paul.

To work as Vincentian is to offer brotherly love, hugs, friendly word and material assistance to the needy. I love being Vincentian and I am very happy to help those who need it.
